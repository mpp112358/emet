# Ejercicio 4.28

 
Resuelve los siguientes sistemas matriciales:

A.   $$ \left\{
\begin{array}{l}
  2X-3Y= \matriz{cc}{1&5\\4&2} \\ X -Y= \matriz{cc}{-1&0\\3&6}
\end{array}
 \right.$$ 
B.   $$ \left\{
\begin{array}{l}
  X+Y= \matriz{cc}{2&1\\3&0} \\ X -Y= \matriz{cc}{6&2\\0&1}
\end{array}
 \right.$$ 
C.   $$ \left\{
\begin{array}{l}
  2X+Y= \matriz{cc}{3&1\\0&-2} \\ X +2Y= \matriz{cc}{1&0\\-2&4}
\end{array}
 \right.$$
