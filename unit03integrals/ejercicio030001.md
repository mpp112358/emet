# Ejercicio 3.1

A.   $$ \displaystyle \int  x \left( 3-x^2 \right) ^3 \; dx$$ 
B.   $$ \displaystyle \int\dfrac{ 1}{ (x-1)^2} \; dx$$ 
C.   $$ \displaystyle \int\dfrac{ x}{ 1+x^2} \; dx$$ 
D.   $$ \displaystyle \int\dfrac{ 2}{ 2+3x^2} \; dx$$ 
E.   $$ \displaystyle \int\dfrac{ x^2+1}{ x} \; dx$$ 
F.   $$ \displaystyle \int\dfrac{ x+1}{ \sqrt{x}} \; dx$$ 
G.   $$ \displaystyle \int\dfrac{ dx}{ \sqrt{9-x^2}}$$
